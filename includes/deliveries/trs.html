🎵 Artist Claiming Process - Simple Guide

A step-by-step guide showing how artists claim their profiles on My Gig Guide.

📋 Quick Overview

What is this? When an admin creates an artist profile for you, you need to "claim" it by registering and verifying your email. This guide shows you exactly how to do that.

Time required: About 5 minutes (plus email verification)

📸 Step 1: Admin Creates Your Profile

What's happening:

An admin on My Gig Guide creates your artist profile but doesn't link it to any user account yet.

What you'll see:

Your artist profile appears on the website, but no one can edit it yet.

What's happening:

When you register on the website, use the SAME email address that's on your artist profile.

What you'll see:

After clicking "Register", you'll see a special message like this:

What's happening:

You'll receive a verification email with special information about claiming your artist profile.

What you'll see in the email:

We found an artist profile 'Your Stage Name' linked to your email address. Once you verify your email, this profile will be automatically claimed and linked to your account!

⏱️ What If There's a Waiting Period?

Sometimes, the system gives a 48-hour "grace period" before claiming your profile. This is to protect you in case someone else tries to claim it fraudulently.

What you'll see:

  • Email says: "Your claim will be processed after 2 days"
  • You'll get another email when it's approved

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I don't get the verification email?

A: Check your spam folder. If it's not there, click "Resend Verification Email" on the verification page.

Q: What if someone else tries to claim my profile?

A: During the grace period (if enabled), you'll get a warning email. You can dispute the claim and an admin will review it.

Q: How do I know which email to use?

A: Use the same email address that was provided when your artist profile was created. This is usually your professional/booking email.

Q: Can I change my email after claiming?

A: Yes! Once your profile is claimed, you can update your email in your profile settings.

📝 Summary

The claiming process is simple:

  1. ✅ Register with the email on your artist profile
  2. ✅ Check your email for verification
  3. ✅ Click "Verify & Claim Profile"
  4. ✅ You're done! Your profile is claimed

That's it! No complicated forms or extra steps. Just register and verify your email.

🛠️ For Admins: Creating Unclaimed Artists

To create an artist profile that can be claimed:

  1. Go to Admin Panel → Artists → Create New
  2. Fill in artist details (Name, Genre, Bio, etc.)
  3. ⭐ Check "Create as Unclaimed Artist"
  4. ⭐ Enter the contact email (required!)
  5. Click Save

The artist profile will now be available for claiming!

Need Help? Contact support if you have issues claiming your profile.
